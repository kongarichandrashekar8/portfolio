Random Questions

Main aim of this project is to help students in their interview prepration

Available in web as well as mobile app

  • Click me for web
  • Click me for app

The main intent of the app is to provide the skills that are required to crack an interview. It is all about giving random questions to you ,based on your interest. This app furnishes you queries from JAVA,SQL,DATA STRUCTURES and OPERATING SYSTEM. App will be giving the checkboxes for you to choose. According to your interest in groundwork, Random questions are generated by clicking on the button “Another” given at the bottom of the page. If you click on a certain language checkbox, this app provides the interview questions in that language. Simply if you choose java, only java based interview questions are being generated. If you choose all the languages, mixture of all the mentioned languages interview questions are generated. This application generates the queries randomly from the languages that are provided. These random questions helps you to think of the question that helps one in increasing their memory power.
